List of Services:

Service Changes

Is your electrical service box/panel out of date or code? Is your electrical demand beginning to be greater than what your current service can provide?

Reach out to us and we can discuss potential changes and upgrades to your residential electrical service in order to meet your needs and requirements.

EV Charging Stations

Are you in need of a greater power supply to charge your electric vehicles? Hoping to avoid having to frequently charge your EV at gas stations and parking lots?

At Schott Industries Incorporated, we can install sleek and efficient EV charging stations at your residence. Reach out to us today in order to hear about our options for electric vehicle charging stations, with a range of available voltage installations.

Lighting Retrofits

If you live in an older home or haven't had your light fixtures retrofitted recently, you may be lighting your home much less efficiently than you could with a modern-day lighting system. With the recent uprising of LED light fixtures, lighting your home can become less power-consuming and more economic than ever before!

We can source and install modern light fixtures in your home, saving you money for years to come. Reach out to us for any lighting retrofitting needs!

Renovations and Rewires

Our electrical service technicians have years of experience with new residential builds. If you are undergoing a renovation or expansion of your home, you can trust us with all of your new elecrtical needs!

For many older homes, out-of-date wire can lead to electrical hazards and decreased efficiency in home electrical consumption. Wires themselves can be made of resistive material such as aluminum, or non-insulating wire sheaths may be in place instead of modern insulating electrical tape. If you think that you are in need of a residential rewire, reach out to us today!
