Millwrighting and Electrical Service Professionals

Who we are:


Schott Industries Incorporated is a local company providing electrical and millwrighting services to clients throughout Southwestern Ontario. With a team consisting of highly skilled electricians and millwrights, we promise to deliver quality solutions to suit your needs.




Sectors we Serve:

RESIDENTIAL ELECTRICAL

Our team offers a number of residential electrical services, including panel changes and upgrades, EV charcing stations, and general renovations.

INDUSTRIAL MILLWRIGHTING

Our team is experienced in maintaining, troubleshooting and servicing recycling equipment, wire tie systems and hydraulic equipment.

INDUSTRIAL ELECTRICAL

For your industrial needs, we provide safety circuit installation, electrical troubleshooting, and general system upgrades.

COMMERCIAL ELECTRICAL

Our services here include lighting retrofits, conduit and wire installation, and emergency system maintanence.



What you can expect from us:

  • Cost effective solutions to your problems
  • Prompt and reliable residential electrical services
  • Flexible service schedules
  • Educated and experienced millwright and electrical technicians
  • In-person and over-the-phone technical support
