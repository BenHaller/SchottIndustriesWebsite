Who we Are:



At the core of Schott Industries Incorporated is a dedicated team with years of collective experience. We are certified to provide electrical services within the residential and commercial industries, with our in-house Master-Licence electrical contractor bringing many years experience. For millwrighting projects, our team carries 15+ years of industrial millwrighting experience, working with machinery from various industrial sectors. With their expertise, our team ensures the highest standards of quality and precision in every project we undertake.

Based in Elmira, Ontario, we extend our services to the cities of Kitchener, Waterloo, Guelph, Cambridge, Brantford, and neighboring municipalities. Our local presence allows us to respond promptly to your electrical and millwrighting needs, providing efficient and reliable solutions.

Schott Industries Incorporated is more than just a service provider; we are a community-oriented company. Our values revolve around tight family bonds, emphasizing the well-being of both our clients and employees. We believe in fostering lasting relationships and contributing positively to the communities we serve.

When you choose Schott Industries Incorporated, you choose expertise, reliability, and a personalized touch. Contact us to hear more about what we can do for you.
