Years of Experience

At Schott Industries Incorporated, our technicians are certified within the commercial electrical field. With years of experience, you can trust us to suit your commercial electrical needs. List of Services:

Commercial Lighting Retrofits

In many older commercial spaces, lighting systems remain in place which draw large amounts of electricity. With our commercial lighting retrofit services, you can replace older, high-draw lighting systems with newer more energy-efficient ones.

Our team is experienced with retrofitting lighting systems at a commercial level, and can begin saving you money as soon as you're ready!

Exit and Emergency Lighting

Within commercial spaces in Ontario, exit and emergency lighting is required to adhere to public guidelines. Our team has experience with the installation and maintanence of these systems, and can help you improve your commercial space by ensuring that your emergency and exit lighting systems are up to date!

Conduit and Wire Installation

Looking at having a new commercial space fitted with electrical services, with appropriate conduit and power supplies fit to your spaces needs? Facing an increase in electrical requirements, and looking to have further electrical wires and conduit run throughout your commmercial space?

Our team has years of experience with commercial wiring, and can help you achieve your goals. Reach out today!
